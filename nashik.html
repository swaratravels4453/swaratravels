Places to Visit in Nashik

Spiritual, heritage and leisure attractions covered by Swara Travels

🛕 Jyotirlinga

Trimbakeshwar Temple

📍 Trimbak, 28 km from Nashik

One of 12 sacred Jyotirlingas — unique 3-faced Shivalinga (Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva). Origin of holy Godavari River. Major Kumbh Mela site every 12 years. Best place for Narayan Nagbali & Kaal Sarp Dosha Puja.

✨ Only 3-faced Jyotirlinga · Source of Godavari · Kumbh Mela site

🌿 Ramayana Site

Panchvati & Ramkund

📍 Panchvati, Nashik City

Where Ram, Sita & Lakshman lived during 14-year exile. Ramkund is a holy ghat on Godavari — ashes immersed here give moksha to departed souls. Five sacred banyan trees.

✨ Ramayana exile site · Moksha for ancestors · Sacred Godavari ghat

🪨 Ancient Cave

Sita Gufa (Sita's Cave)

📍 Panchvati, Nashik

Ancient cave where Goddess Sita stayed during Ramayana exile. Ram-Sita-Lakshman temple inside. Narrow cave passage — unique devotional experience. Near the spot Ravana abducted Sita.

✨ Sita's cave from Ramayana · Inside Ram-Sita-Lakshman temple · Unique narrow entry

🖤 Historic Temple

Kalaram Temple

📍 Panchvati, Nashik City

270-year-old temple with rare black stone idol of Lord Ram. Historic — Dr. B.R. Ambedkar led landmark 1930 satyagraha here for Dalit temple entry rights. Beautiful black stone architecture.

✨ Rare black Ram idol · 270 year old · Ambedkar 1930 Satyagraha site

⛰️ Shakti Peeth

Saptashrungi Devi Temple

📍 Vani, 65 km from Nashik

Powerful Shakti Peeth of Maharashtra at 4600 ft height. 8-foot natural rock idol with 18 hands. 470 steps pilgrimage climb. Stunning Sahyadri mountain views from the top.

✨ Major Shakti Peeth · 8-foot 18-hand rock idol · 4600 ft mountain temple

🍇 Wine Tourism

Sula Vineyards

📍 Gaulane, 15 km from Nashik

India's most famous winery. Nashik is India's "Wine Capital." Vineyard tours, wine tasting sessions, lake-view resort. Annual SulaFest music festival. Perfect leisure trip addition.

✨ India's #1 winery · Wine tasting & tours · Annual SulaFest festival

🏛️ White Marble Temple

Muktidham Mandir

📍 Nashik City

Stunning pure white Rajasthani marble temple — entire Bhagavad Geeta carved on walls. Replicas of all 12 Jyotirlingas under one roof. Dedicated to Lord Krishna and the Geeta.

✨ Geeta carved on marble walls · All 12 Jyotirlingas replicas · Stunning architecture

🏔️ Heritage Caves

Pandavleni Caves

📍 Nashik outskirts, 8 km from city

24 ancient Buddhist caves (250 BC–600 AD). Beautiful carvings, stupas and inscriptions. Short rewarding hike to panoramic view of Nashik city. Best heritage site in Maharashtra.

✨ 250 BC Buddhist caves · 24 caves with carvings · Panoramic Nashik view

Can I visit both Nashik and Shirdi in same trip? ▼

How far is Nashik from Mumbai and how long does it take? ▼
Nashik is approximately 165–170 km from Mumbai via NH-3 (Mumbai-Nashik Highway). Travel time by cab is around 3–3.5 hours in normal traffic. Swara Travels provides comfortable AC cab for this popular outstation route.
