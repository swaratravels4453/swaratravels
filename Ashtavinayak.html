Ashtavinayak means "Eight Ganeshas" — 8 ancient Swayambhu Ganesha temples across Maharashtra. Yatra starts & ends at Morgaon. Swara Travels provides fixed-fare cab with no surge pricing — better than Uber for temple tours.

The 8 Ashtavinayak Temples

Visit in this sequence for complete Yatra

Temple 1 · Start & End

Moreshwar — Morgaon

📍 Morgaon, Pune Dist · 55 km from Pune

Most important of all 8 — both start and end of Yatra. Only 3-eyed Ganesha idol, trunk turned left. Patronized by Peshwas.

✨ Only 3-eyed Ganesha · Peshwa patronized

Temple 2

Siddhivinayak — Siddhatek

📍 Siddhatek, Ahmednagar · 100 km from Pune

Only Ashtavinayak with trunk turned RIGHT — extremely rare & powerful. Built by Queen Ahilyabai Holkar on Bhima River banks.

✨ Trunk-right idol · Ahilyabai Holkar built

📲 Book Cab
Temple 3

Ballaleshwar — Pali

📍 Pali, Raigad Dist · 120 km from Pune

Only Ganesha temple named after a devotee (Ballal). Diamond-studded eyes. Sun rays fall on idol during winter solstice.

✨ Named after devotee · Diamond eyes · Sun aligned

📲 Book Cab
Temple 4

Varadvinayak — Mahad

📍 Mahad, Raigad Dist · 146 km from Pune

"Fulfiller of wishes." Sacred Nandadeep lamp burning continuously since 1892 — 130+ years! Swayambhu idol found in lake.

✨ Nandadeep burning since 1892 · Swayambhu idol

📲 Book Cab
Temple 5

Chintamani — Theur

📍 Theur, Pune Dist · 25 km from Pune

"Remover of all worries." Closest Ashtavinayak to Pune. At confluence of Mula, Mutha & Bhima rivers. Peshwa association.

✨ Closest to Pune · 3 rivers · Peshwa association

📲 Book Cab
Temple 6

Girijatmaj — Lenyadri

📍 Lenyadri, Pune Dist · 94 km from Pune

Only Ashtavinayak inside a mountain cave. 307 steps climb. Idol carved in rock — can't be moved. Lit only by sunlight. Shivneri Fort nearby.

✨ Only cave temple · 307 steps · Shivneri nearby

📲 Book Cab
Temple 7

Vighnahar — Ozar

📍 Ozar, Pune Dist · 95 km from Pune

"Remover of obstacles." Gold-plated pinnacle visible from far. Diamond forehead. On Pune-Nashik Highway near Kukadi River.

✨ Gold pinnacle · Diamond forehead · Pune-Nashik Hwy

📲 Book Cab
Temple 8 · Most Powerful

Mahaganapati — Ranjangaon

📍 Ranjangaon, Pune Dist · 50 km from Pune

Most powerful of all 8. Lord Shiva worshipped here. Hidden idol has 10 trunks & 20 hands (Mahotkat). Built in 9th–10th century.

✨ Most powerful · 10-trunk hidden idol · Shiva worshipped
