Ashtavinayak means "Eight Ganeshas" — 8 ancient Swayambhu (self-manifested) temples of Lord Ganesha spread across Maharashtra. The pilgrimage begins and ends at Morgaon. Completing this Yatra is believed to fulfill all wishes. Swara Travels provides comfortable, fixed-fare cab for the complete Ashtavinayak circuit from Mumbai, Pune, or Nashik — no surge pricing, no app needed, WhatsApp booking.

The 8 Ashtavinayak Temples

Visit in this sequence for complete Ashtavinayak Yatra

Temple 1 · Start & End

Moreshwar (Mayureshwar) — Morgaon

📍 Morgaon, Pune District · 55 km from Pune

Most important Ashtavinayak temple — both starting and ending point of the Yatra. Only 3-eyed Ganesha idol, trunk turned left. Patronized by the Peshwas.

✨ Only 3-eyed Ganesha · Trunk turned left · Peshwa patronized

Temple 2

Siddhivinayak — Siddhatek

📍 Siddhatek, Ahmednagar District · 100 km from Pune

Only Ashtavinayak temple where the idol's trunk is turned to the right — extremely rare and powerful. Built by Queen Ahilyabai Holkar on Bhima River banks.

✨ Only trunk-right Ashtavinayak · Vishnu worshipped here · Ahilyabai Holkar built

📲 Book Cab for This Temple
Temple 3

Ballaleshwar — Pali

📍 Pali, Raigad District · 120 km from Pune

Only Ganesha temple in the world named after a devotee. Diamond-studded eyes. Sun rays fall directly on the idol during winter solstice — ancient architectural marvel.

✨ Named after devotee Ballal · Diamond-studded eyes · Sun-aligned architecture

📲 Book Cab for This Temple
Temple 4

Varadvinayak — Mahad

📍 Mahad (Kolad), Raigad District · 146 km from Pune

"Fulfiller of all wishes." The sacred Nandadeep oil lamp has been burning continuously since 1892 — over 130 years without stopping. Swayambhu idol found in lake.

✨ Nandadeep burning since 1892 · Swayambhu idol · Devotees enter inner sanctum

📲 Book Cab for This Temple
Temple 5

Chintamani — Theur

📍 Theur, Pune District · 25 km from Pune

"Remover of all worries." Located at the confluence of Mula, Mutha and Bhima rivers. Closest Ashtavinayak to Pune. Associated with Peshwa rulers.

✨ Closest to Pune · 3 rivers confluence · Peshwa association

📲 Book Cab for This Temple
Temple 6

Girijatmaj — Lenyadri

📍 Lenyadri (Junnar), Pune District · 94 km from Pune

Only Ashtavinayak inside a mountain cave. 307 steps pilgrimage climb. Idol carved in rock — cannot be moved. Lit only by sunlight, no electricity. Shivaji's birthplace (Shivneri Fort) nearby.

✨ Only cave temple · 307 steps · Sunlight-only lighting · Shivneri Fort nearby

📲 Book Cab for This Temple
Temple 7

Vighnahar (Vighneshwar) — Ozar

📍 Ozar (Junnar), Pune District · 95 km from Pune

"Remover of all obstacles." Gold-plated pinnacle visible from far. Diamond-studded forehead. Fort-like high stone walls. Located on Pune-Nashik Highway near scenic Kukadi River.

✨ Gold-plated pinnacle · Diamond forehead · Pune-Nashik Highway location

Temple 8 · Most Powerful

Mahaganapati — Ranjangaon

📍 Ranjangaon, Pune District · 50 km from Pune

Most powerful of all 8 Ashtavinayak. Lord Shiva worshipped here. Hidden idol has 10 trunks & 20 hands (Mahotkat) — shown only on special occasions. Built in 9th–10th century.

✨ Most powerful Ashtavinayak · Hidden 10-trunk idol · Lord Shiva worshipped here
