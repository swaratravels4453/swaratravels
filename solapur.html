🙏 Maharashtra's Varanasi

Vitthal Rukmini Temple — Pandharpur

📍 Pandharpur · 75 km from Solapur · 340 km from Mumbai

Pandharpur is the spiritual capital of Maharashtra — home of Lord Vitthal (Krishna). Millions of devotees take the Wari pilgrimage here for Ashadhi Ekadashi and Kartiki Ekadashi. The Bhima River (Chandrabhaga) ghat here is sacred for ancestor rituals.

✨ Spiritual capital of Maharashtra · Ashadhi Ekadashi Wari · Chandrabhaga holy ghat

🔱 Swami Samarth

Swami Samarth Temple — Akkalkot

📍 Akkalkot · 40 km from Solapur

Dedicated to Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj — believed to be an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya. Swami lived in Akkalkot for many years performing miracles. Devotees believe visiting Akkalkot removes life problems and fulfills wishes. One of the most powerful spiritual destinations in Maharashtra.

✨ Incarnation of Lord Dattatreya · Powerful miracle-performing Swami · Major devotee following

⚔️ Shakti Peeth

Tulja Bhavani Temple — Tuljapur

📍 Tuljapur · 45 km from Solapur

One of the 51 sacred Shakti Peethas of India. Tulja Bhavani is the family deity (Kuladevata) of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj — he sought her blessings before every battle. Thousands of devotees visit for Navratri and Diwali festivals. The powerful goddess is believed to grant courage and protection.

✨ Shivaji Maharaj's Kuladevata · 51 Shakti Peethas · Navratri major festival

🏛️ Lake Temple

Siddharameshwar Temple — Solapur

📍 Solapur City · Siddheshwar Lake

Lord Shiva temple on an island in Siddheshwar Lake — one of the most beautiful temple settings in Maharashtra. Built by Saint Siddharama. Huge Mahashivratri fair held here every year. Peaceful, scenic spiritual spot right in Solapur city.

✨ Island temple on lake · Saint Siddharama built · Mahashivratri grand fair
