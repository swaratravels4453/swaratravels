Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about outstation cab booking in Maharashtra

How to book outstation cab with Swara Travels? ▼
Simply WhatsApp us at 8104050420 or fill the online booking form above. Tell us your pickup city, destination, travel date and car preference. We confirm your booking with a fixed fare — no app needed, no surge pricing.
Is Swara Travels cheaper than Uber outstation? ▼
Yes. Uber and Ola charge surge pricing during weekends, festivals (Ganesh Chaturthi, Ashadhi Ekadashi, Diwali) and peak hours. Swara Travels gives you a fixed outstation rate decided before your trip starts — no hidden charges, no surprises.
Do you cover Ashtavinayak Yatra from Mumbai? ▼
Yes! We specialize in Ashtavinayak Yatra from Mumbai and Pune. The full 654 km circuit covering all 8 temples (Morgaon, Siddhatek, Pali, Mahad, Theur, Lenyadri, Ozar, Ranjangaon) is available in a 2-day comfortable package. WhatsApp us for pricing.
What are your cab rates per km? ▼
Our outstation rates vary by car type and distance. Sedan (Dzire/Xcent) starts at ₹12–14/km and 6-seater SUV (Ertiga/Carens) at ₹16–18/km for outstation trips. All rates are fixed — no surge ever. WhatsApp for exact quote for your route.
Do you have cabs available during Ashadhi Ekadashi / Pandharpur Wari? ▼
Yes, we specifically ensure availability during major Wari seasons and festivals. Unlike Uber/Ola which may show surge or unavailability, Swara Travels has pre-booked slots for Ashadhi Ekadashi, Kartiki Ekadashi and other pilgrimage seasons. Book in advance to confirm your slot.
Can I book one-way outstation cab? ▼
Yes. We offer both one-way and round-trip outstation packages. One-way cab from Mumbai to Nashik, Mumbai to Pune, or Mumbai to Solapur is available at best rates. WhatsApp us to get your one-way fare.
